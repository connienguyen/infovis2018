Immigration to Sweden

Instructions

Explore different data on immigrants in Sweden. In the graphs below we present country specific data on all immigrants from these countries.

You can click the countries and see relevant facts relating to migration from this country to Sweden. For certain data you will find personal stories behind the data.

Definitions

International migrant: A person who is living in a country other than his or her country of birth.

Internal migrant: A person who is living in the same country as his or her country of birth but at a different location in this country.

Refugee: Person fleeing armed conflict or persecution.

Migrant: A person who choose to move not because of a direct threat of persecution or death, but mainly to improve their lives by finding work, or in some cases for education, family reunion, or other reasons.
